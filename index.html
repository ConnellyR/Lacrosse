Lacrosse

Lacrosse or LAX for short has three positions. Offence defence and middle. Then there are sub sections of those postions, for example defence front. These sub sections mean were a player stands on the field,

Defence Offence
Front Blue Red
Back Green Orange
Right Purple Yellow
Left Lime Pink

There are some other LAX terms to learn such as

  • the eight
  • the twelve
  • the crease
  • the top
  • " I got your help"
  • "I got ball"
  • " Crash"

Now you have learned some basic lacrosse info. :)
